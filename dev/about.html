About Us

Urbansoundprague provides a foundation for all you electronic music lovers, club enthusiastics, dancing queens and boys and all others who want to enter the world of Techno and Technotronic in Prague.

Czech out first whats going on around the clubs in Praha on our home page or "Techno Praha" facebook site (link above). We update every week and search for the freshest and coolest events, underground clubs and beats.

While on "Techno Praha", you might consider to hit the LIKE Button to show support and love to us, because we are a non-profit, non-bullshit organization. While you suppport us, we have plans and updates coming, so stay tuned for more on urbansoundprague.com.

We have fun. We love techno. We want the same for you. Holla at us.
